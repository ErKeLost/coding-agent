Click an element inside an existing browser session.

Provide a structured target such as:
- `selector`
- `text`
- `role` + `name`
- `placeholder`
- `label`

Use this when a browser step needs a real click, such as expanding menus, switching tabs, submitting forms, or navigating single-page applications.

Always reuse the same `sessionId` / `session_id` from `browser_open`.
